Personal Information We Collect

The Application accesses or collects the following categories of Personal Information:

Device or Other IDs

The Application collects and uses Device or Other IDs, specifically:

  • Device ID — a unique identifier associated with your device, used for analytics and ad serving purposes
  • Advertising ID — accessed via the AD_ID permission and Google AdServices, used by AdMob to serve personalized advertisements

Photos

The Application accesses Photos stored on your device. This access occurs via the READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, and READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permissions. Photos are accessed only when you explicitly select an image to scan or convert to PDF. Photos are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded, stored on our servers, or shared with any third party.

Videos

The Application may access Videos stored on your device as part of Android's broad storage access granted by the READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions. The Application does not actively use, read, upload, or collect video content. Video access is an incidental result of the storage permissions required for PDF creation and file saving features.

Voice or Sound Recordings

The Application may access Voice or Sound Recordings on your device as part of Android's broad storage access granted by the READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions. The Application does not actively use, read, upload, or collect audio or voice recording content. This access is an incidental result of the storage permissions required for core PDF functionality.

Files and Documents

The Application accesses Files and Documents on your device when you choose to import them for PDF conversion. Only files you explicitly select are accessed. Files are processed locally and are not uploaded, stored on our servers, or shared with any third party.

Usage and Analytics Data

The Application also collects the following non-personal usage data:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Pages of the Application you visit, time and date of your visit, time spent on pages
  • Total time spent on the Application
  • The operating system used on your mobile device
  • App interaction data — buttons clicked and features used
  • Crash logs — error and crash reports to improve app stability
  • Coarse location — approximate location derived from your IP address for analytics

What We Do NOT Collect

The Application does not collect: precise location, phone number, user account information, contacts, text messages, installed apps list, health information, or sensitive personal information. The Application does not use AI technologies to process your data.

App Permissions

The Application requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • CAMERA — To scan physical documents using your device camera. Only activated when you explicitly start a scan. Not used for any other purpose.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — To import existing images, files, and documents from your device for PDF conversion. Grants access to Photos, Videos, and Voice or Sound Recordings as part of Android storage access, though only documents and images you select are actively used.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — To save generated PDF files to your device. Grants write access to Photos, Videos, and Voice or Sound Recordings as part of Android storage access, though only PDF output files are written by the app.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — To allow gallery image selection on Android 13+. Only images you explicitly select are accessed.
  • INTERNET — To load advertisements via AdMob and send anonymous analytics/crash data.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — To check network connectivity before loading ads or sending analytics.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — To keep PDF processing running in the foreground during large document conversions.
  • WAKE_LOCK — To prevent the screen from sleeping during long scans or PDF conversions.
  • AD_ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) — To access the Advertising ID for serving personalized ads via AdMob.
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_AD_ID — Used by Google AdServices SDK for ad identification on Android 13+.
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_ATTRIBUTION — Used by Google AdServices SDK to measure ad campaign effectiveness.
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_TOPICS — Used by Google AdServices SDK to serve interest-based ads via Android's Privacy Sandbox Topics API, which runs on-device and does not share browsing history.
