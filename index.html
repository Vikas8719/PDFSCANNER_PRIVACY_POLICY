Personal Information We Collect

The Application collects and processes the following categories of Personal Information:

  • IP Address — your device Internet Protocol address

2. Photos

  • Photos are accessed from your device storage via READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions for the purpose of scanning and converting to PDF format

3. Videos

  • Videos may be accessed from your device storage via READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions

4. Voice or Sound Recordings

  • Voice or Sound Recordings may be accessed from your device storage via READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions

5. Music Files

  • Music files may be accessible from device storage via READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission

6. Other Audio Files

  • Other audio files may be accessible from device storage via READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission

7. Files and Documents

  • Files and documents are accessed from your device storage for importing and converting to PDF format

8. App Usage and Technical Data

  • Operating system and device information
  • Network state and connectivity information
  • App interaction data — buttons clicked, features used, time spent
  • Crash logs and error reports to improve app stability
  • Ad interaction data — ad clicks, impressions, and performance data

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to process your personal data.

App Permissions

The Application requests the following permissions:

  • CAMERA — To scan documents using your device camera
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — To import Photos, Videos, Voice Recordings, Files and Documents for PDF conversion
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — To save converted PDF files to your device; may also access Photos, Videos, Voice or Sound Recordings
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — To read image files on newer Android versions
  • INTERNET — For ad serving and analytics
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — To check network connectivity for ad loading
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — For background document processing
  • WAKE_LOCK — To keep device awake during document processing
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_AD_ID — Used by Google Ad Services for advertising
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_ATTRIBUTION — Used by Google Ad Services for measuring ad performance
  • ACCESS_ADSERVICES_TOPICS — Used by Google Ad Services for interest-based advertising
